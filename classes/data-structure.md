    <h2>Data Structure</h2>
    <p class="section-text">
      The Data Structure course introduces fundamental data structures and
      algorithms that support efficient computation, with a focus on
      <strong>implementation</strong>, <strong>complexity</strong>, and
      <strong>practical usage</strong>.
    </p>

    <h3>Main Topics</h3>
    <ul class="section-text">
      <li>Arrays, linked lists, stacks, and queues</li>
      <li>Trees, binary search trees, and heaps</li>
      <li>Hash tables and dictionaries</li>
      <li>Basic graph structures and traversal algorithms</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Course Features</h3>
    <ul class="section-text">
      <li>Coding assignments in C/C++ or Python</li>
      <li>Analysis of time and space complexity</li>
      <li>Examples connected to real systems and CPS</li>
    </ul>

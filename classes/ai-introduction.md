# AI Introduction

This course provides an overview of **core AI and machine learning concepts**,  
with examples drawn from real applications in perception, decision making,  
and robotics.

## Core Contents
- Search, reasoning, and basic planning  
- Supervised and unsupervised learning  
- Evaluation, generalization, and overfitting  
- Brief introduction to deep learning and LLMs  

## Teaching Style
- Concept-oriented lectures with intuitive examples  
- Python-based assignments and small projects  
- Connections to safety, robustness, and ethics in AI
